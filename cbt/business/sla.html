6. Exclusions

The following are NOT covered by this SLA:

  • Hardware failures (servers, network switches, UPS)
  • Internet service provider (ISP) outages
  • Power supply interruptions
  • Unauthorised modifications to the software by the Client or third parties
  • Issues arising from Client's failure to apply recommended security updates
  • Force majeure events

7. SLA Credits

If Firmvision fails to meet availability targets due to causes within its control, the Client may claim service credits as follows:
