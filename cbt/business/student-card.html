CBT Exam — Rules & Tips

Babcock University High School · Read Before Your Exam

EXAM RULES

🖥

Stay Fullscreen
Do not exit fullscreen during the exam. Exiting counts as a violation.

🚫

No Tab Switching
Do not open other browser tabs or apps. Every switch is detected and logged.

⏱

Watch the Timer
The countdown is at the top. When time runs out, your exam is auto-submitted.

💾

Auto-Saved
Every answer saves instantly. You can move between questions freely.

📵

No Phone Use
No calls, messages, or searching during the exam. Invigilator can see violations.

🔄

Power Cut?
Log back in on the same device. Your answers are saved and timer continues.

⚠ Violations: Exiting fullscreen or switching tabs counts as a violation. After 3 violations your exam is automatically submitted with whatever you have answered.

TIPS FOR SUCCESS

  • Read every question carefully before selecting an answer
  • Use the question grid (sidebar) to track unanswered questions
  • Flag difficult questions and return to them at the end
  • For multi-select: select ALL correct options, not just one
  • For essay: write in full sentences and cover the key points
  • Submit only when you are ready — submission is final

🙋 Problem during exam? Raise your hand immediately — do NOT attempt to fix technical issues yourself. The invigilator will contact IT support.

Good luck! Do your best. 🌟
