Exam Integrity

Four Layers of
Anti-Cheat Protection

🖥

Fullscreen Lock

Exam runs in mandatory fullscreen. Exiting triggers a violation warning. Three violations = auto-submission.

🔍

Tab-Switch Detection

Any browser tab switch or window minimise is detected in real time and logged with timestamp.

🔀

Question Randomisation

Questions and answer options are shuffled uniquely per student. No two students see the same order.

🎫

One-Time Session Tokens

Cryptographic 256-bit token per exam session. Cannot be shared, replayed, or used by another student.
